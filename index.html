dat-gateway

portal to a decentralized web

You can use this gateway to visit content hosted over the p2p Dat protocol.

So why not visit something?

Enter Dat keys in the URL like this: /:key/:path

Example: /garbados.hashbase.io/icons/favicon.ico

Well, there it is right now!

You can also use Dat software to share things of your own by installing Dat and sharing archives.

Happy sharing, nerds!
